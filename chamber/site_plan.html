Website Site Plan

Prepared by AKUMU

1. Site Name

Site Name: RapidsQuest Adventures

Reason: This name evokes excitement, adventure, and the thrill of white-water rafting while being memorable and easy to brand. It suggests a quest or journey through rapids, appealing to adventure seekers.

2. Site Purpose

The primary purpose of RapidsQuest Adventures is to provide information and booking services for white-water rafting trips. Specifically, the site will:

  • Showcase available rafting trips with details on difficulty levels, locations, durations, and prices.
  • Allow users to book trips online and view availability calendars.
  • Share safety information, guide bios, customer testimonials, and photos/videos from past trips to build trust and excitement.
  • Educate visitors about white-water rafting and promote environmental responsibility on rivers.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1 – Young adventure seeker (Target: 18-30 years old): Alex is a college student looking for a fun weekend activity with friends. Alex searches "rafting trips near me" and wants to quickly see trip options by difficulty (beginner to advanced), prices, and available dates. Alex needs clear photos and a simple booking button to reserve spots without too many steps.

Scenario 2 – Family planner (Target: 30-50 years old with kids): Maria is planning a family vacation and wants safe, beginner-friendly rafting. She needs to find family-oriented trips, read about safety measures, see age requirements, and read reviews/testimonials from other families. Maria also wants contact info for questions and an easy way to inquire about group discounts.

4. Color Schema

At least two main colors are chosen to evoke energy, nature, and trust:

  • Primary Color: #2c7bb6 (Bright Blue) – Used for headers, main navigation links, section titles, and accents to represent water/rivers and build trust.
  • Secondary Color: #fdae61 (Warm Orange) – Used for call-to-action buttons (e.g., "Book Now"), highlights, and icons to convey energy, excitement, and adventure.

5. Color Schema Use in Document

This site plan document demonstrates the color schema: blue is used for headings and accents, orange appears in highlights (e.g., borders on wireframes), and the overall design reflects the planned website aesthetic.

6. Typography Named and Used

Selected Fonts:

  • Headings: Merriweather (serif) – Bold and classic, used for main titles (h1, h2, h3) to give a professional yet adventurous feel. It is applied here in all headings.
  • Body Text: Roboto (sans-serif) – Clean, modern, and highly readable, used for all paragraphs, lists, and general content. It is the default font for the body in this document.

These Google Fonts are linked and actively used throughout this site plan.
