Site Plan – ALMA CREATORS CHAMBER

Site Name

ALMA CREATORS CHAMBER – This name reflects a hub for commerce, culture, and creativity in Kampala.

Optional domain: kampalacreatorschamber.org

Site Purpose

The site highlights Kampala’s cultural and commercial landmarks, providing visitors with information about museums, heritage sites, markets, and places of worship. It serves as a discovery hub for both locals and tourists.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find cultural attractions to visit in Kampala?
  • What historical sites are recognized as UNESCO World Heritage in the city?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #e67e22 (Orange) – Used for highlights, buttons, and accents.

Secondary Color: #2c3e50 (Deep Navy) – Used for headings and navigation.

Neutral Background: #f9f9f9 – Used for page background.

Typography

  • Georgia, serif – Used for headings to emphasize cultural richness.
  • Arial, sans-serif – Used for body text for readability and clarity.
